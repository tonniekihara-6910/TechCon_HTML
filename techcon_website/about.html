About TechCon 2024

Discover the story behind the world's premier technology conference and our mission to drive innovation forward.

Our History

From humble beginnings to global impact

TechCon was founded in 2010 by a group of visionary technologists who believed in the power of collaboration to drive innovation. What started as a small gathering of 200 enthusiasts in a San Francisco warehouse has grown into one of the world's most influential technology conferences.

Over the past 14 years, TechCon has hosted over 50,000 attendees, featured more than 500 industry-leading speakers, and facilitated countless partnerships that have shaped the technological landscape.

Our Mission

Driving technological progress through collaboration

At TechCon, our mission is to create a platform where innovators, thought leaders, and technology enthusiasts can connect, share knowledge, and inspire the next wave of technological breakthroughs.

We believe that technology should be accessible, ethical, and beneficial to all of humanity. Our conferences are designed to:

  • Foster cross-disciplinary collaboration
  • Showcase cutting-edge innovations
  • Address the ethical implications of new technologies
  • Promote diversity and inclusion in tech
  • Inspire the next generation of technologists

Through our events, we aim to not only showcase what's possible today but also to envision and work toward a better technological future.

Notable Past Speakers

Industry leaders who have shaped TechCon's legacy

Over the years, TechCon has been privileged to host some of the most influential voices in technology. These visionaries have shared their insights, challenged conventional thinking, and inspired thousands of attendees.

Our speaker alumni include Nobel laureates, Turing Award winners, founders of billion-dollar companies, and researchers pushing the boundaries of what's possible with technology.

Below are just a few of the notable speakers who have graced our stages in previous years.

Dr. Elena Rodriguez

AI Research Director, NeuroTech Labs

Pioneer in neural network applications for medical diagnostics. Her 2021 keynote on ethical AI influenced industry standards worldwide.

Marcus Chen

CTO, QuantumLeap Computing

Led the development of the first commercially viable quantum processor. His 2019 presentation demystified quantum computing for thousands.

Dr. Amina Jallow

Founder, GreenTech Solutions

Revolutionized sustainable technology with her work on carbon-capture systems. Her 2022 talk inspired a wave of eco-tech startups.

James O'Connell

Cybersecurity Expert, former White House Advisor

His 2020 presentation on global cybersecurity threats led to new international cooperation frameworks in digital defense.

Sophie Williams

VR/AR Pioneer, Immersive Tech Labs

Transformed digital interaction with her groundbreaking work in mixed reality. Her 2018 demo set new standards for immersive experiences.

Dr. Kenji Tanaka

Robotics Innovator, Future Automation Inc.

His work on human-robot collaboration has redefined manufacturing and service industries. The 2023 robotics showcase was a conference highlight.
